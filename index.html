Are You Ready To Test Your Luck In A Game Of Rock, Paper, Scissors?

How To Play

A simple and fun game is "rock, paper, scissors." It was a game that a lot of us used to play in school to settle conflicts or pass the time. You won't be competing against a human opponent this time; instead, you'll be up against a computer. For those who have never played, the rules are quite simple:

  • If you choose Rock, you will win against Scissors but lose against Paper.
  • If you choose Scissors, you will win against Paper but lose against Rock.
  • If you choose Paper, you will win against Rock but lose against Scissors.

Try your luck right now and see if you can defeat the computer.
